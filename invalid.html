When is :invalid applied?

In my opinion, Webkit/534 applies the :invalid pseudo-class far too early. As soon as you start typing in an email field, it’s “invalid”. As soon as the page loads, <input required /> fields are “invalid.”

This is like telling the user they are wrong, that they have made a mistake.

They haven’t.

The only thing they did was open a page with HTML5 input fields.
